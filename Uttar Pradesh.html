Tourist Destinations in Uttar Pradesh
Taj Mahal

Visit the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, known for its stunning architecture and romantic history.

Varanasi Ghats

Explore the spiritual city of Varanasi, known for its ancient ghats along the Ganges River, vibrant culture, and religious significance.

Fatehpur Sikri

Visit the historical city of Fatehpur Sikri, an architectural marvel and former Mughal capital, showcasing beautiful palaces, mosques, and courtyards.
