Tourist Destinations in Chhattisgarh
Chitrakot Waterfall

Located near Jagdalpur, it's the widest waterfall in India, often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of India."

Kailash Gufa

A naturally formed cave located near Raigarh, known for its unique rock formations and religious significance.

Bhoramdeo Temple

A complex of Hindu temples in Kabirdham, known for its architectural beauty and intricate carvings.

Muktangan Temple

A Jain pilgrimage site in Rajnandgaon, known for its scenic surroundings and spiritual ambiance.
