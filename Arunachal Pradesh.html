Tourist Destinations in Arunachal Pradesh
Tawang Monastery

The largest monastery in India and an important center for Mahayana Buddhism, located in Tawang district.

Sela Lake

A breathtaking high-altitude lake situated near Sela Pass, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Namdapha National Park

A biodiversity hotspot and the largest protected area in the Eastern Himalaya biodiversity hotspot.

Bomdila Monastery

A Buddhist monastery in West Kameng district, known for its serene ambiance and panoramic views.
