Tourist Destinations in Lakshadweep
Lakshadweep Islands

A group of stunning coral islands located in the Arabian Sea, known for pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and rich marine life.

Lakshadweep Beaches

Spectacular sandy beaches with turquoise waters, ideal for water sports, snorkeling, and diving amidst pristine natural beauty.

Kavaratti Island

The capital of Lakshadweep, featuring pristine lagoons, coconut palm groves, and beautiful coral reefs ideal for snorkeling and diving.

Lakshadweep Lagoons

Explore the picturesque lagoons of Lakshadweep, offering tranquility and opportunities for water activities amidst breathtaking scenery.
