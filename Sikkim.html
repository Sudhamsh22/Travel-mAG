Tourist Destinations in Sikkim
Kanchenjunga

Explore the stunning views of the third highest mountain peak in the world, Kanchenjunga, located on the border of Sikkim and Nepal.

Pemayangtse Monastery

Visit this ancient Buddhist monastery known for its stunning architecture and serene surroundings in Pelling, Sikkim.

Gurudongmar Lake

Witness the beauty of one of the highest lakes in the world, Gurudongmar Lake, nestled in the Himalayas at an altitude of over 17,000 feet.
