Tourist Destinations in Punjab
Golden Temple, Amritsar

The iconic Sikh pilgrimage site known for its stunning architecture, spiritual significance, and serene surroundings.

Wagah Border Ceremony

Experience the thrilling border ceremony between India and Pakistan, known for its patriotic fervor and grand spectacle.

Ranjit Sagar Dam (Thein Dam)

A picturesque dam on the Ravi River offering boating, water sports, and scenic views of the surrounding landscape.

Anandpur Sahib

A historic Sikh town with renowned gurdwaras and a rich cultural heritage, especially during the Hola Mohalla festival.
