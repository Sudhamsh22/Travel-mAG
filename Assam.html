Tourist Destinations in Assam
Kaziranga National Park

Famous wildlife sanctuary known for one-horned rhinoceros, tigers, elephants, and extensive birdlife.

Kamakhya Temple

Ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kamakhya, located on Nilachal Hill in Guwahati.

Majuli Island

Largest river island in the world located on the Brahmaputra River, known for its unique culture and natural beauty.

Assam Tea Gardens

Scenic tea estates spread across the hills of Assam, offering breathtaking views and tea plantation experiences.
