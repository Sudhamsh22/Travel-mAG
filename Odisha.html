Tourist Destinations in Odisha
Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its architectural grandeur and sun chariot sculpture.

Dhauli Shanti Stupa

A historic Buddhist monument with serene surroundings and panoramic views of the Daya River.

Nandankanan Zoological Park

A popular zoo and botanical garden known for its rare white tigers and extensive flora collection.

Puri Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Odisha, famous for its golden sands, vibrant festivals, and water sports.
