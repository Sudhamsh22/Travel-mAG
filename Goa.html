Tourist Destinations in Goa
Calangute Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its golden sand, water sports, and vibrant nightlife.

Vagator Beach

Scenic beach known for its dramatic red cliffs, stunning sunset views, and laid-back atmosphere.

Se Cathedral

Historic cathedral in Old Goa, dedicated to St. Catherine, featuring stunning Portuguese architecture.

Arambol Beach

Relaxed beach popular among backpackers, known for its serene vibes, live music, and alternative culture.
