Tourist Destinations in Jammu and Kashmir
Dal Lake

A stunning lake in Srinagar, famous for its houseboats, shikara rides, and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Gulmarg

A popular hill station and skiing destination, offering stunning views of snow-capped peaks, lush green meadows, and adventure activities.

Pahalgam

A scenic town in Anantnag district, known for its lush green valleys, pristine rivers, and as a base for the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

Pangong Lake

An enchanting lake situated in Ladakh, famous for its ever-changing colors, crystal-clear waters, and scenic landscapes.
