Tourist Destinations in Delhi
India Gate

A prominent landmark and war memorial in Delhi, offering a scenic view and a popular spot for evening strolls.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world, showcasing intricate Islamic architecture.

Lotus Temple

An architectural marvel known for its lotus-like shape, serving as a Bahá'í House of Worship and meditation space.

Akshardham Temple

A stunning temple complex showcasing traditional Hindu and Indian architectural styles, featuring exhibitions and cultural shows.
