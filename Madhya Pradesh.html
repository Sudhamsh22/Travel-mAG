Tourist Destinations in Madhya Pradesh
Khajuraho Temples

Famous for its stunning ancient temples known for their intricate erotic sculptures and architectural brilliance.

Bhimbetka Caves

An archaeological site exhibiting prehistoric rock shelters with ancient cave paintings, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Omkareshwar Temple

A revered Hindu temple located on an island in the Narmada River, dedicated to Lord Shiva, with a scenic riverside setting.

Gwalior Fort

A historic hill fort known for its imposing architecture and panoramic views, situated in the city of Gwalior.
