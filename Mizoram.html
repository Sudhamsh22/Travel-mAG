Tourist Destinations in Mizoram
Phawngpui National Park

A scenic national park known for its rich biodiversity and beautiful landscapes, also called the "Blue Mountain."

Vantawng Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in Mizoram, a stunning natural attraction surrounded by lush greenery.

Champhai

A picturesque town known for its natural beauty, vibrant local culture, and scenic views of the surrounding hills.

Murlen National Park

A wildlife sanctuary and national park offering opportunities for trekking and wildlife spotting.
