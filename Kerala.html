Tourist Destinations in Kerala
Munnar

Hill station known for its tea plantations, lush greenery, and beautiful landscapes.

Alleppey Backwaters

Famous for its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and traditional Kerala hospitality.

Kovalam Beach

Renowned beach destination with palm-lined shores and pristine waters, ideal for relaxation and water activities.

Athirappilly Waterfalls

Spectacular waterfall surrounded by lush forests, often referred to as the "Niagara of India."
