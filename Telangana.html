Tourist Destinations in Telangana
Charminar

Iconic monument and mosque located in the heart of Hyderabad, known for its historic significance and architecture.

Golkonda Fort

A majestic fort complex renowned for its impressive acoustic architecture and panoramic views of Hyderabad.

Basar Temple

A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, located near Nizamabad, attracting devotees and tourists alike.

Ramappa Temple

An ancient temple recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, celebrated for its exquisite architecture and sculptures.
