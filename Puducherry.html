Tourist Destinations in Puducherry
Rock Beach

Promenade beach with rocks and statues, offering a scenic view of the sea and a popular spot for morning walks and sunset views.

Paradise Beach

Secluded beach accessible by boat from Chunnambar Boat House, known for its pristine shores and tranquility.

Auroville

International township known for its unique architecture, spiritual community, and the Matrimandir, a golden spherical temple.

Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Jesus

Gothic-style Catholic church with stained glass windows, located in the heart of Puducherry, attracting visitors for its architecture and religious significance.
