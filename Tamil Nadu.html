Tourist Destinations in Tamil Nadu
Mahabalipuram Beach

Picturesque beach near Chennai known for its shore temples, stone carvings, and scenic coastline.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

Historic Hindu temple in Madurai dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and known for its towering gopurams and intricate sculptures.

Ooty (Udhagamandalam)

Popular hill station nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, offering scenic landscapes, tea gardens, and pleasant weather.

Rameswaram Temple

Sacred temple town on Pamban Island known for its Ramanathaswamy Temple and scenic beaches.
