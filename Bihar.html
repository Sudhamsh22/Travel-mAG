Tourist Destinations in Bihar
Mahabodhi Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Bodh Gaya, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment, attracting Buddhist pilgrims from around the world.

Rajgir

A historic city near Nalanda known for its ancient ruins, hot springs, and Buddhist attractions like Gridhakuta Hill and Venuvana.

Patna Museum

A prominent museum in Patna housing a rich collection of artifacts and relics dating back to ancient and medieval periods of Indian history.

Kesariya Stupa

One of the largest and oldest Buddhist stupas in the world, located in Champaran district, known for its historical and architectural significance.
