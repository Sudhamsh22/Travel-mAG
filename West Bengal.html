Tourist Destinations in West Bengal
Victoria Memorial

Explore the iconic Victoria Memorial in Kolkata, a magnificent marble structure exhibiting Indo-Saracenic architecture and housing a museum.

Darjeeling

Visit the charming hill station of Darjeeling, known for its tea gardens, panoramic views of Mount Kanchenjunga, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

Sundarbans

Embark on an adventure to the Sundarbans, the world's largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for its Royal Bengal Tigers.
