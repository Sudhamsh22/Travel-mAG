Tourist Destinations in Andhra Pradesh
Kanaka Durga Temple

A famous Hindu temple located in Vijayawada, dedicated to Goddess Durga, offering scenic views of the city.

Tirumala Temple

An ancient temple located in Tirupati, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, attracting millions of pilgrims every year.

Borra Caves

Natural limestone caves located near Visakhapatnam, known for stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations.

Araku Valley

A picturesque hill station in the Eastern Ghats, known for its coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture.
