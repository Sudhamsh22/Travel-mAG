Tourist Destinations in Manipur
Kangla Fort

Historical fort complex in Imphal, representing the rich cultural heritage of Manipur.

Loktak Lake

Largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, known for its floating islands (phumdis) and biodiversity.

Thalon Caves

Ancient limestone caves near Tamenglong, offering unique spelunking experiences for adventure seekers.

Kaina Hill

Sacred hill in Moirang, famous for the ancient Kaina Temple dedicated to Goddess Kali.
