Tourist Destinations in Chandigarh
Rock Garden

An extraordinary garden made from industrial and home waste, showcasing sculptures and artworks by Nek Chand.

Sukhna Lake

A picturesque man-made lake at the foothills of the Himalayas, ideal for boating and enjoying scenic views.

Capitol Complex

An architectural masterpiece designed by Le Corbusier, housing government buildings and monuments.

Rose Garden

Asia's largest rose garden featuring thousands of rose varieties, making it a popular attraction for nature lovers.
