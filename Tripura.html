Tourist Destinations in Tripura
Ujjayanta Palace

Explore the grandeur of Ujjayanta Palace, a former royal palace and now a museum showcasing Tripura's cultural heritage.

Neermahal Palace

Visit Neermahal, a stunning palace built in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake, known for its picturesque setting and unique architecture.

Jampui Hills

Explore the scenic beauty of Jampui Hills, famous for its orange orchards, panoramic views, and vibrant tribal culture.
