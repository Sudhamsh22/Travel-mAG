Tourist Destinations in Ladakh
Pangong Lake

A mesmerizing high-altitude lake situated in the Himalayas, known for its scenic beauty and changing colors.

Nubra Valley

A breathtaking valley surrounded by snow-capped mountains, offering camel rides on sand dunes and hot springs.

Lamayuru Monastery

One of the oldest and largest monasteries in Ladakh, perched atop a hill and surrounded by stunning landscapes.

Tso Moriri Lake

A serene high-altitude lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains and home to diverse bird species.
